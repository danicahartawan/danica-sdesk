About ⌘

Hi, I'm Danica.

I always thought I'd become a behavior technician — and in a way, I did (fun fact: I'm a licensed behavior technician for autism). I've long been fascinated by how people think, learn, and make decisions, and that curiosity now shapes the systems I build.

Outside of work, I teach a class at Berkeley on Alzheimer's and neurotechnology (EDSTEM98!), journal obsessively, care a lot about self-growth, attempt to cycle more, collect ceramic mugs and glassware, wander through art spaces, and document my life on Substack and YouTube.
